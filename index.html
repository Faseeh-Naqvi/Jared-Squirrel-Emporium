About Us

Named after the adopted father of our founder, Jared's Squirrel Emporium is dedicated to connecting squirrel enthusiasts with the quirkiest and most adorable squirrels in the world. If you're looking for a friendly companion, we've got you covered. Founded on the principles of love for squirrels and care for their well-being, our emporium strives to bring joy to every squirrel lover's life.

A playful squirrel enjoying its life.

Meet Our Team

Faseeh N.

Founder & Squirrel Enthusiast

Faseeh started this emporium in 2004 to share his lifelong passion for squirrels. At the age of 19, he was found by a family of squirrels and raised by them. His mission is to help people find their perfect squirrel companions.

Alex T.

Operations Manager

Alex is the powerhouse of the company. During Lunch recess and after school, she ensures everything runs smoothly at the emporium. From inventory management to customer satisfaction, Alex's relentless dedication keeps our squirrels and customers happy.

Jackson E.

Creative Director

Jackson's creativity is behind our wonderful branding and marketing. He loves designing campaigns that capture the unique personalities of our squirrel companions. His enthusiasm and creativity inspires us all.
